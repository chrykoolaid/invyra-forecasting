# Forecast Types

## Phase 1

Demand forecast uses moving average, weighted moving average, and trend adjustment.

Days of cover estimates how long available stock can support forecast demand.

Stockout risk considers days of cover, supplier lead time, demand velocity, safety stock, and confidence.

Overstock risk considers days of cover, slow movement, demand weakness, and excess quantity beyond target cover.

Reorder recommendation uses forecast demand during lead time, safety stock, current available stock, minimum order quantity, and rounding.

## Future

Supplier reliability, expiry pressure, markdown timing, cross-location replenishment, new store opening stock, category demand, and finance/procurement planning forecasts.
