# Integration Map

Forecasting should feed existing Invyra modules, not replace them.

Inventory: item details forecast/risk, demand forecast, days cover, stockout/overstock risk, confidence, warnings.

Reorder Review: suggested quantity, reason, risk level, confidence.

Purchasing: purchase urgency, supplier lead-time impact, draft PO recommendation support, no auto-purchasing.

ScanOps: Gap Scan validation support, Floor Scan variance interpretation, shelf risk insight.

Dashboard: priority inventory issues, stockout risk summary, overstock risk summary, low-confidence warnings.

Reports: forecast accuracy, stockout prevention, overstock risk, supplier reliability.

Suppliers: supplier lead-time visibility and future supplier delay risk inputs.
